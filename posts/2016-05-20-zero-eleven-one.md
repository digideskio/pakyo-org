---
title: "Pakyow v0.11.1 Release Announcement"
time: 1:50pm CST
---

Pakyow v0.11.1 is now available on RubyGems. This release fixes a bug where
`Pakyow::Helpers` was not included into `Pakyow::App`. It impacted the ability
to complete the warmup tutorial, and caused issues in other apps as well. No
more!
