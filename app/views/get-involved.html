--- title: Pakyow › Get Involved ---

Technology is only as good as the people behind it.
We'd love to have you.

We're glad you're interested in getting involved with Pakyow. Here are some ways you get jump in.

Give us a star on GitHub. Don't stop there – share what we're up to with your friends!

Follow @pakyow on Twitter. We also post on Facebook and Google+.

Ask and answer questions on Stack Overflow. They're tagged, so it's easy!

Found a bug or have an idea for a feature? Post it to our issues page.

Join the mailing list, where early discussions about new Pakyow features take place. You can also might find some of us on the #pakyow channel on irc.freenode.net.

Want to contribute code or documentation? Post to the mailing list and we'll do our best to find a place where you can contribute.
