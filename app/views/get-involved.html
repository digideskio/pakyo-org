--- title: Pakyow › Get Involved ---

Help build a better web framework while creating a great community.

Please be kind. This guides our interaction as a community. We don't just want a better way to build for the web, we want to create a positive community that's accessible to everyone.
Read our full code of conduct.

Show Your Support

Give us a star on GitHub. But don't stop there – tell your friends about the project! Writing blog posts, giving talks to local user groups, and screencasting your experiences with Pakyow all help spread the word. We'll return the favor by helping promote the things you create. Also consider adding a link back to pakyow.org from your project.

Join the Conversation

Come hang out on our Gitter channel, which took the place of our IRC channel in 2015. You should also join the forums, where early discussions about new Pakyow features often take place. Don't forget to lend a hand – ask and answer questions on Stack Overflow.

Contribute to the Project

We'd love your contributions to Pakyow code or documentation. Everything is hosted publicly on GitHub. Looking through the list of open issues would be a great place to start. If you need more direction, jump on one of the communication channels listed above; we're happy to help!

New to Pakyow or open-source but want to get involved? We try to make it a little easier by labeling starter issues. These are usually smaller issues that touch a small amount of code; great for getting more familiar with the project!

Report Problems you Encounter

Found a bug or have an idea for a new feature? Open up an issue on our issues page.
